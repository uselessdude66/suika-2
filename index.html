Ado Watermelon Game

The Ado Watermelon Game, once called Suika Game, is making a big splash in the gaming world. It's a puzzle video game from Aladdin X, a Japanese developer. This game is all about falling and merging puzzles, and it's getting people all over the world excited. It all started with an idea from a Chinese browser game called "Synthetic Big Watermelon." But it wasn't until December 9, 2021, that Aladdin X released it on Nintendo eShop in Japan. That's when it became the global sensation we now know as the Watermelon Game.

About Ado Watermelon Game

In the Ado Watermelon Game, the goal is simple, but it’s super fun. You have to put different fruits into a box one by one. What makes it cool is how these fruits move around inside the box. As you add fruits, they roll and shift, making the game exciting.

Stacking Fruits: You need to be smart about how you put the fruits into the box. The game’s rules decide how the fruits move. You have to plan carefully so the box doesn’t fill up too much and end the game. Also Play Suika Merge Melons and Suika Bottle Watermelon

Merging Fruits: The real fun starts when the same kinds of fruits touch each other. Instead of just stacking, they come together to make bigger fruits. This creates a cool chain reaction, making the game more thrilling. You have to think fast and plan your moves.

The Watermelon: The best part of the game is the Watermelon. It gives you the most points, and it takes up a lot of space in the box when you put it in. This makes the game even more exciting because you have to decide when to use the Watermelon for the most points. It’s like a risk-and-reward challenge.

So, in this game, you stack fruits, make them merge, and aim for the Watermelon to score big. It’s easy to understand and tons of fun!
